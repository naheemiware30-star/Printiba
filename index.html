About Printiba

Built for Nigeria's
print industry

Founded in Lagos, Printiba was built to solve a problem every Nigerian printer knows — calculating a job cost accurately and quickly without spreadsheets, guesswork or long phone calls. We combined print industry expertise with modern technology to create Nigeria's first intelligent print production suite.

Our mission is to empower printers, designers and businesses to quote faster, produce better and run more profitable print operations.
